Privacy Policy for ZenPocket

Last updated: January 2025

COLO, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ZenPocket mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Username: When you create an account, you choose a username
  • Comments: Any comments or reflections you post on daily koans
  • Chat Messages: Conversations you have with the AI Zen Chat feature
  • Chat History: Your saved chat sessions and conversations

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Identifier: A unique ID for your device (to maintain your account)
  • Crash Reports: Basic diagnostic data if the app crashes (via Apple's services)
  • App Version: Which version of ZenPocket you're using

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Real names or email addresses
  • Location data
  • Personal photos or media
  • Browsing history
  • Contact lists

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Display your comments to other users
  • Provide AI-powered chat responses and maintain chat history
  • Improve our AI chat responses and app features
  • Prevent spam and abuse
  • Fix bugs and improve the App
  • Manage your subscription (handled by Apple)

Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely in our database
  • Chat conversations are private to your account
  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • Only you can delete your account and data
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties

AI Chat Feature

When you use the AI Zen Chat:

  • Your messages are processed to generate responses
  • Chat history is saved to your account for continuity
  • Conversations are private and not shared with other users
  • You can delete individual chats or all chat history
  • We may use anonymized chat data to improve the AI service

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data associated with your account in the App
  • Delete: Remove your account at any time through My Page > Delete Account
  • Control: Delete individual chats or clear all chat history
  • Opt-out: Choose not to use features that collect data

When You Delete Your Account

  • Your username and device ID are removed immediately
  • Your chat history is permanently deleted
  • Your existing comments remain but show as "Deleted User"
  • We keep a record of the deletion for 30 days to prevent abuse
  • Subscriptions must be cancelled separately through iPhone Settings

Children's Privacy

ZenPocket is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information beyond usernames, comments, and chat messages, which are the same for all users regardless of age. Parents should supervise children's use of the chat feature.

Subscription Information

  • Subscriptions are processed by Apple
  • We do not have access to your payment information
  • Apple's privacy policy applies to payment processing

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Apple Services: For crash reporting and subscriptions
  • Database Hosting: For storing user data securely
  • AI Services: For powering the Zen Chat feature (when implemented)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
